# How to Create Your Account

**Category:** Getting Started  
**Last updated:** [Date]

---

Welcome to NovaTech! Creating your account takes less than 2 minutes. Follow the steps below to get started.

## Steps

1. Go to [app.novatech.com/signup](https://app.novatech.com/signup)
2. Enter your **full name**, **work email address**, and a **password** (minimum 8 characters)
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Check your inbox for a verification email from `noreply@novatech.com`
5. Click the **Verify Email** button in the email
6. You'll be redirected to your new NovaTech dashboard

## Didn't receive the verification email?

- Check your spam or junk folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email address
- Wait 2–3 minutes and try again
- Click **Resend Verification Email** on the login page
