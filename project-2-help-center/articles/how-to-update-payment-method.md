# How to Update Your Payment Method

**Category:** Billing & Account  
**Last updated:** [Date]

---

You can update your payment method at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.

## Steps

1. Log in to your NovaTech account
2. Click your **profile icon** in the top-right corner
3. Select **Account Settings**
4. Navigate to the **Billing** tab
5. Click **Update Payment Method**
6. Enter your new card details and click **Save**

Your next invoice will be charged to the updated payment method.

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- ACH Bank Transfer (Enterprise plans only)

## Need to update your billing address?

On the same Billing tab, click **Edit Billing Address** and update your information there.
