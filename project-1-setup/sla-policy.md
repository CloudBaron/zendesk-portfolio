# SLA Policy — NovaTech Support

## Overview

This SLA policy is designed for a small-to-mid-size SaaS support team.
Priority levels are set by the customer at ticket creation and can be escalated by agents.

---

## Tiers

### Urgent
- **Who it applies to:** System outages, complete product failure, data loss risk
- **First Reply:** 1 hour
- **Next Reply:** 2 hours
- **Resolution:** 8 hours
- **Rationale:** Business-critical issues that block the customer entirely. Every minute down costs revenue.

### Normal
- **Who it applies to:** Bugs, feature issues, partial outages
- **First Reply:** 4 hours
- **Next Reply:** 8 hours
- **Resolution:** 24 hours
- **Rationale:** Standard issues that affect workflow but have workarounds available.

### Low
- **Who it applies to:** General questions, how-to requests, feedback
- **First Reply:** 8 hours
- **Next Reply:** 24 hours
- **Resolution:** 72 hours
- **Rationale:** Non-urgent requests that can be batched and handled during slower periods.
